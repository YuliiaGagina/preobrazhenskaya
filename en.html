ABOUT ME

My name is Yulia Pryobrazhenska, I was born in Ukraine, in the city of Nova Kakhovka. I have dedicated my entire conscious life to photography and painting.

My peak of transformation happened when I moved to Bali at the age of 20. At that time, my camera broke due to the high humidity on the island, and being abroad for the first time, I was left alone without work or money.

This was the strongest catalyst for my creativity: "Creating with nothing."

I photograph the female body and portrait. Separate works reveal the characters and individuality of the people being photographed, but there are also those where I embody my ideas, using the female body as clay.

“

Inspired by the difference in values and cultures, I challenge the stereotypical perception of the female body. I want you to take a new, fresh look at the familiar.

And, by looking at my work, find the answer to the question: “What defines beauty today?”
